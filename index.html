THE STORY

The Synopsis

ANIREEKSHITHAA is a short film about the unexpected twists life throws at us. Moments that seem frustrating, suffocating, or unfair at first often reveal their true meaning with time.

Through the journey of Tejas, the film explores how life's unplanned events shape us in ways we never anticipate. As he navigates a series of unforeseen situations, he gradually realizes that every setback, encounter, and coincidence has a purpose.

In the end, Tejas comes to believe that life unfolds according to a greater design—that everything happens for a reason, and we are all simply players in a game guided by a higher force.

ANIREEKSHITHAA is a reflection on fate, faith, and the beauty hidden within life's uncertainties.
